Organization Behavior

Organization is the group of people who work together to achieve defined goal,vision and mission like Indian Army,Indian Railway,Tata Motors,Amrapali Group of Institutes etc .We can use the word organization to prefer the group or business. Organization behavior is the study of human behavior in both,group as well as an individual in an organization. Keith Davis said that-Organization behavior is the study and application of knowlegde about how people act within a organization.
